05 — Design Philosophy

How I approach
human-AI interaction design.

01

Understand the user's mental model

AI interactions fail when they don't match how users think. I start with research to map intent, vocabulary, and expectation gaps.

02

Define the AI's character & boundaries

Every AI product needs a persona: what it knows, how it sounds, what it won't do. I establish these foundations before writing a single response.

03

Design the full turn-by-turn arc

Great conversational design is architecture. I map every branch — the happy path, the clarification loop, the graceful failure — before polishing copy.

04

Test, measure, and iterate

I validate with real users and track concrete metrics — task success rates, escalation frequency, satisfaction scores — to drive continuous improvement.
